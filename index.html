Sinani Youth Community Centre empowers young people by providing education, support, and opportunities to address challenges like teenage pregnancy, gender-based violence, and substance abuse. We strive to create a safe environment where youth can learn, grow, and become positive leaders in their communities.

Empowering Girls Through Education and Support

Sinani Youth Community Centre runs a Girls Empowerment Club to inspire, educate, and support young women. We provide mentorship, life skills training, and a safe space for girls to discuss challenges, build confidence, and become leaders in their communities.
